Privacy Policy — World Master

Effective: 17 June 2026 · Developer: Vector Labs · Contact: apps.vector.labs@gmail.com

World Master is an offline-first geography learning app. We collect as little as possible. This page explains exactly what leaves your device, when, and why.

1. The app works offline

All learning, quizzes, maps, scoring, streaks, and progress are stored only on your device. You can use the entire app with no internet connection and with all permissions denied. The network is used only for these optional, clearly-labelled features:

FeatureWhat is sentWhy
Global leaderboardA device-generated player ID, your chosen display name, your scoreTo rank you against other players
Arcade (multiplayer)The same player ID, display name, score, in-match movesTo run a live match
Anonymous usage analytics (opt-out)Coarse, non-identifying gameplay events, app version, device typeTo improve the app
Sign in with Google (optional)Your Google email + basic profile, via Firebase AuthenticationOnly if you create an account to back up progress

If you never open the leaderboard/arcade, never sign in, and turn analytics off, nothing leaves your device.

2. What we do NOT collect

No advertising, ad SDKs, or ad identifiers. No location, contacts, microphone, or camera tracking. No selling or sharing of personal data. No cross-app tracking.

3. Identifiers

To keep your leaderboard position stable, the app generates a random player ID (on Android anchored one-way to ANDROID_ID). It is not your name, email, or phone number, and is never used for advertising.

4. Data deletion

  • Uninstalling the app removes all local data from your device.
  • In-app: if you created an account, open Profile → Delete account to permanently erase your account, leaderboard scores, and profile from our servers.
  • By request: email apps.vector.labs@gmail.com with your display name (or account email) and we will delete your server-side data within 30 days.

5. Analytics opt-out

Open Profile and turn off “Share anonymous usage data”. No usage events leave your device after that.

6. Accounts & Google sign-in

Sign-in is optional, powered by Google Firebase Authentication. See Google’s policy: policies.google.com/privacy.

7. Children

This app is intended for users aged 13 and over. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13.

8. Security & retention

Network traffic uses standard transport encryption (HTTPS/TLS). Analytics events are deleted after about 13 months. Leaderboard/profile data is kept while the app operates, or until you delete it.

9. Changes

If this policy changes, the effective date above is updated and the new version published at this URL.

10. Contact

Questions or deletion requests: apps.vector.labs@gmail.com
