Delete Your Account & Data — World Master

Developer: Vector Labs · App: World Master (com.worldgeo.world_geography_master) · Contact: apps.vector.labs@gmail.com

This page explains how to delete your World Master account and the data associated with it. You can play World Master fully anonymously; an account is optional and only exists if you signed in.

Option 1 — Delete in the app (instant)

  1. Open World Master.
  2. Go to Profile.
  3. Tap Delete account and confirm.

This permanently erases your account and all server-side data immediately.

Option 2 — Request by email

Send an email to apps.vector.labs@gmail.com with the subject “Delete my account” and include your display name (or the account email you signed in with). We delete your server-side data within 30 days.

Local data on your device

Uninstalling the app removes all local data (progress, settings, cached scores) from your device.

Retention

Once deleted, data is not recoverable. Aggregated, non-identifying statistics that cannot be linked back to you may be retained.
